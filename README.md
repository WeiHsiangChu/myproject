## 📘 Course Projects (RL & ML)
- **[MNIST Patch K-Means](graduate/course-projects/mnist-patch-kmeans/)**  
  K-means on **5×5** MNIST patches; cluster evolution for \(K=100\to10{,}000\), reconstruction with patch-wise normalized MSE (curve + recon grids).

- **[Non-Parametric Data Synthesis](graduate/course-projects/non-parametric-data-synthesis/)**  
  Efros & Leung (1999) texture synthesis (32×32, 64×64) + MNIST “8” extension; results shown as combined panels for consistent sizing.

- **[Few-Shot Representation Learning](graduate/course-projects/few-shot-representation-learning/)**  
  Self-supervised pretraining on Oxford 102 Flowers. Compared **SimCLR** vs **MAE** with linear/MLP probes; results reported with **Results** as the final section.

- **[Gesture Detection (Contrastive Learning)](graduate/course-projects/gesture-detection-contrastive-learning/)**  
  SimCLR on a two-class gesture subset (**circle** vs **eight**) using preprocessed sequence features; compared **X_feat**, **X_crop**, **X_resampling** augmentations (resampling performed best).

- **[MCES Blackjack](graduate/course-projects/mces-blackjack/)**  
  Monte Carlo Exploring Starts for Blackjack; reproduced policy \(\pi^*\) and value \(V^*\) under two dealer soft-17 behaviors (hit/stick) with usable vs no-usable-ace plots.

- **[Cliffwalking TD](graduate/course-projects/cliffwalking-td/)**  
  TD control in the 4×12 Cliffwalking domain; **Sarsa**, **Q-learning**, **Expected Sarsa** with performance vs episodes.

- **[Random Walk](graduate/course-projects/random-walk/)**  
  1000-state random walk; **Gradient Monte Carlo** vs **Semi-Gradient TD(0)** with state aggregation; true \(v_\pi\) by iterative policy evaluation.

- **[Multi-Armed Bandit](graduate/course-projects/multiarmed-bandit/)**  
  Epsilon-greedy vs **UCB** on k-armed bandits; reproduces the textbook comparison figure.

- **[Coin Toss — Optimal Policy](graduate/course-projects/coin-toss-optimal-policy/)**  
  Optimal stopping policy analysis for a coin-toss setup.

- **[Coin Toss — Policy Evaluation](graduate/course-projects/coin-toss-policy-evaluation/)**  
  Empirical evaluation of candidate policies for the coin-toss problem.
